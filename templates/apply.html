HOW TO APPLY

1.LOGIN

Login to your account

2.KYC VERIFICATION

Each customer has to submit their KYC Document to the bank, and get verified. Upon successful verification, bank issues a unique KYC Security Number, called as KN.

3.GOLD DEPOSIT VERIFICATION

KYC Completed Customer, should raise an Application request for Gold Loan, by entering the details.After submission, the customer is given with the Request id(RID), for the proof for submission. All Details are verified and would get rejected for review in case if some thing founds to be incorrect.

4.DEPOSIT GOLD IN BANK

The customer has to submit the gold to the nearest Star Finance office against the RID. Then Gold Verification is done by the bank and stores the Gold Quality Index against the RID. Bank sends a notification to the Customer with the application status (accepted/rejected) etc, with amount it can disburse of the bullion deposited

5.GET THE LOAN

The customer can view, and opt either accept or reject the bank offer. In case of accept, the money is credited to the Customer Bank Account. In Case of reject, the customer can go back to the bank, and collect the Gold Ornaments after paying the required service fee (around 500 Rs)
